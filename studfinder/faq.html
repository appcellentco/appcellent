How does Stud Finder work?

Stud Finder uses your iPhone's built-in magnetometer to detect magnetic fields created by metal fasteners (nails or screws) in wall studs. When you move your device across a wall, the app detects changes in the magnetic field and alerts you when a stud is detected.

Which devices are compatible with Stud Finder?

Stud Finder is compatible with iPhone models that have a magnetometer sensor. This includes most iPhone models from iPhone 6 and newer. The app requires iOS 15.0 or later.

How accurate is the stud detection?

Stud Finder provides reliable detection in most standard wall constructions. However, accuracy can be affected by:

  • Wall thickness and material composition
  • Presence of metal pipes, wiring, or other objects
  • Distance between the device and the wall surface
  • Environmental magnetic interference

For professional construction work, we recommend verifying measurements with professional-grade equipment.

Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

No, Stud Finder works completely offline. All stud detection happens locally on your device using the magnetometer sensor. An internet connection is only required for initial download and updates.

Why isn't the app detecting studs?

If you're having trouble detecting studs, try the following:

  • Ensure your iPhone case doesn't contain metal or magnets
  • Remove any magnetic accessories from your phone
  • Hold the device flat against the wall
  • Move slowly and steadily across the wall surface
  • Calibrate the app by moving away from the wall and returning
  • Check that you've granted necessary sensor permissions
Does the app work on all wall types?

Stud Finder works best on standard drywall with wood or metal studs. Performance may vary on:

  • Plaster walls
  • Concrete or brick walls
  • Walls with extensive metal lath
  • Walls with significant electrical wiring or plumbing
How do I use the level feature?

The level feature (available in premium) helps you ensure perfect alignment:

  • Tap the level icon in the app
  • Place your iPhone against the surface you want to level
  • The app will show you the angle and provide visual feedback
  • Adjust until the indicator shows level (0 degrees)
