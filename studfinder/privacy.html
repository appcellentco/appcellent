Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

At Appcellent, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Stud Finder ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Stud Finder does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the core features of the app.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect certain information automatically when you use the app, including:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Sensor Data: Magnetometer readings used solely for stud detection functionality (not stored or transmitted)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To improve and optimize the user experience
  • To analyze app usage and performance
  • To provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Sensor data used for stud detection is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Stud Finder may use third-party services for analytics and payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store: For in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Analytics Services: To understand app usage and improve performance

5. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

All in-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Stud Finder is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to object to processing of your information

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

contact@appcellent.co
