Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 07, 2024

1. Introduction

At Appcellent ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit our website or use our mobile applications.

2. Information We Collect

Our applications are designed to minimize data collection. We generally do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it to us (e.g., through support requests).

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve our apps.
  • Device Data: Information such as device type and operating system version may be collected for diagnostics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our applications.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our apps.

4. Data Security

We have implemented measures designed to secure your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@appcellent.co
