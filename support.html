Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore purchases?

Go to the settings menu within the app and tap on "Restore Purchases". Ensure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used to make the purchase.

The app is crashing on launch.

Please try reinstalling the app. If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version.
